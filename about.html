Navigating the Site

An initiative aimed at educating people on mental health, we have provided a host of resources on the website. Using the navigation bar at the top, you can access the various information we provide.

Home

The home page is what you'll first see. It's where you can access all of the other pages from. To emphasize the reason behind our mission, it also contains a variety of mental health statistics in relation to adolescents.

About

The about page, which you are currently one, has information on our mission and vision. It is the page discussing what exactly Project Sunflower is about. Along with this, it includes details on how to navigate the site

Illnesses

The illnesses page is separated in different subpages, each on a different illness afflicting adolescents. Because of this, you can look for a certain illness you want information on and go to that page.

Hotlines

The hotlines page has an assortment of different hotlines. Some may be for all illnesses, but there are also ones geared towards a specific issue. The purpose of each hotline will be labeled as such.

Flyers

The flyers page will have downloadable flyers on different illnesses. These flyers are designed by Project Sunflower and are open to use by everyone. With them, we hope that you can help the initiative and increase mental health literacy in your community!
